Determine Business ObjectiveBackgroundThe project aims to develop a comprehensive model to predict student success by evaluating academic performance and assessing health-related factors. This dual approach will provide a holistic view of students, linking their academic potential with their overall well-being, and potentially guide interventions that address both academic and health needs.
3
Business Objective- Create a predictive model to classify students' grades based on academic and lifestyle factors.
- Develop a health ranking system to assess students’ mental and physical health using health-related metrics.
4
Business Success Criteria- High accuracy in predicting students' academic performance and health rankings.
- Actionable insights that can be used by educators and counselors to improve student support.
